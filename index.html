DAI LUC DONG NAI

Founded in 2017, Dai Luc Dong Nai, is a professional footwear manufacturer specializing in OEM andODM solutions for international brands. With a management and technical team averaging over 20 years of industry experience we deliver reliable, scalable, and compliant production for global markets.

Our story

Dai Luc Dong Nai was established in 2017. Specializing in the manufacturing and export of footwear products with employees over 20 years experiences in footwear industry

Dai Luc has built a solid reputation in the industry by combining modern technology with skilled labor to produce high-quality shoes for various markets worldwide

For over a decade, we have continuously invested in people, technology, and processes to ensure that every pair of shoes that leaves our factory meets the highest standards of quality, durability, and aesthetics.
